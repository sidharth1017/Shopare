what is Shopare?
what made Rudraksh Todwal build a platform that can change the buying habits of consumers?

Shopare is a platform that is created by Rudraksh , so that the consumer can be benefitted and can buy products like groceries , electronics etc. online or can do drive thru (or Take Away) from your nearest stores.

Values at Shopare:

Mission:

Empowering the traditional or physical stores to be a part of revolution in Jaipur
By delivering delightfull experience online and with a new system of Drive Thru or Take Away.

Vision:

Shopare vision is to provide online facilities to every traditional shops for their growth
And vision to cover most of the sectors in market like groceries, medical and apparel etc.
